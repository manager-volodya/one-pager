Plurals — iGaming Entertainment App
Building a TikTok-style app that distributes iGaming content into a personalized short-video feed — where every scroll surfaces a new game, every swipe opens a live stream, and every session creates a natural path to real-money play

I've been watching a new category emerge: TikTok-style discovery feeds for games. Companies like Minit Games just raised $2M to do this for casual mobile gaming (Sony is among their investors). But nobody has built this for #iGaming — yet.

We're building Plurals — and the iGaming version of this idea is fundamentally different, more defensible, and significantly higher-value per user.

What is Plurals?

Imagine TikTok — but instead of music and dance, every video is a slot game teaser, a crash game clip, or a live win moment. Instead of scrolling to a casino lobby with 4,000 games and zero context, you enter a personalised feed that shows you exactly the right game at exactly the right moment.

And here's the part that makes it different from anything else in #iGaming: each scroll is psychologically the same motion as pulling a slot lever. Variable reward, built into the UX itself.

  • ⬆️
    Scroll: New game teaser auto-plays. 15–30 seconds of pure slot gameplay. A personalised algo surfaces the right game for you — high-vol, bonus-buy, crash, or minigame.
  • 📡
    Swipe Left — Go Live: Watch a real streamer (or AI-streamer) play that exact game in real time. Chat, reactions, big wins. Social proof that converts skeptics into players.
  • 👉
    Swipe Right — Instant Demo: The game launches in demo mode immediately. No download, no account, no friction. Try it. Fall in love with it.
  • 💰
    One Tap — Real Money Play: Tap the CTA and you're redirected to a licensed operator partner to play for real. This is where Plurals earns — on every converted player.
